How much do apartments in Serenia District cost? +
Serenia District apartments start from AED 1.86 million ($506,000 USD) for 1-bedroom units (825-899 sq.ft.). 2-bedroom apartments start at AED 3.5 million, 3-bedroom at AED 6.5 million, and 4-bedroom penthouses are available on request. All units include premium finishes and balcony space.
When will Serenia District be completed? +
Serenia District Phase 1 (West Residences) is scheduled for handover in December 2028. The development is currently off-plan with construction underway. This timeline offers investors 2+ years of capital appreciation potential before completion.
Can foreign investors buy property in Serenia District Dubai? +
Yes, Dubai allows 100% foreign ownership in freehold areas including Jumeirah Heights. Investors from Netherlands, Belgium, UK, USA (California, New York), and all other countries can purchase property in Serenia District with full ownership rights, registration at Dubai Land Department, and inheritance protection.
What returns can I expect from Serenia District investment? +
Serenia District offers projected 15-25% capital appreciation by the December 2028 handover, based on Jumeirah Islands area growth of 31.9% in 2024 and comparable off-plan premiums. Post-handover rental yields are projected at 6-8% annually, significantly higher than Amsterdam (3-4%), London (3-4%), or New York (3-4%).
What is the payment plan for Serenia District? +
Serenia District offers a 60/40 payment plan: 10% on booking, 10% within 30 days, 10% June 2025, 10% January 2026, 10% January 2027, 10% June 2027, and 40% on handover (December 2028). This structure minimizes capital commitment during construction while maximizing leverage for appreciation.
Can I get UAE residency by buying in Serenia District? +
Yes, investments starting at AED 2 million qualify for the UAE Golden Visa, offering 10-year renewable residency for the investor, spouse, and children. This includes the right to live, work, and study in the UAE, sponsor domestic staff, and establish businesses with 100% ownership.
