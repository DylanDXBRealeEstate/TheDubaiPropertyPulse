The Golden Visa: 10-Year Residency Through Property Investment

The UAE's Golden Visa program has transformed Dubai from a property investment destination into a lifestyle upgrade for global citizens. Property investments starting at AED 2 million (approximately $545,000) qualify investors for the 10-year renewable visa.

Tax Benefits: Dubai offers zero income tax, zero capital gains tax, and zero property tax. For high-net-worth individuals from high-tax jurisdictions, this represents savings of 40-50% annually.
