Why January 2026 Marks a New Investment Era in Dubai

February 18, 2026 | Market Report

January 2026 didn't just start the year strong for Dubai real estate‚Äîit shattered records. With total transaction values hitting an unprecedented AED 72.4 billion (approximately $19.7 billion), the market achieved its highest monthly sales value in the emirate's history.

This represents a staggering 63% year-on-year increase, signaling that Dubai has evolved from a regional player to a global investment powerhouse.

The Golden Visa: 10-Year Residency Through Property Investment

February 10, 2026 | Legal & Tax

The UAE's Golden Visa program has transformed Dubai from a property investment destination into a lifestyle upgrade for global citizens. Property investments starting at AED 2 million (approximately $545,000) qualify investors for the 10-year renewable visa.

Tax Benefits: Dubai offers zero income tax, zero capital gains tax, and zero property tax. For high-net-worth individuals from high-tax jurisdictions, this represents savings of 40-50% annually.

Family Inclusion: The Golden Visa extends to spouse, children (regardless of age if unmarried), and even domestic staff. It's not just an investment visa‚Äîit's a family relocation solution.
